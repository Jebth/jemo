Welcome to Explore \u2728

Here's how everything works in this space.

\u2665 Relish

The heart button on each card lets you relish someone's memo space. It's like a follower system — show appreciation. Start by relishing Je!

\u2605 Associate

The star button associates you with someone. Associated users get pinned to your explore view. Some accounts are curtained — you must associate with them before you can send memos or view their space.

🔒 Curtained Accounts

Curtained cards are blurred and locked. Click the star to request association — the request goes to their inbox. Once they accept (or it's mutual), the curtain lifts.

\u2709\ufe0f Send a Memo

Click Send \u2192 on any card to drop someone a memo. You can attach images and share your location too.

📄 View Public Memos

Click View on a card to browse their public memo box — memos they've chosen to share with everyone.
